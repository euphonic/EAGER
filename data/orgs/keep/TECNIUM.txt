Tecnium: Pioneers in the handling of corrosive fluids
Founded in 1957 in Manresa (Barcelona), born to meet a need in the industry: finding solutions to handling corrosive liquids and gases.
Innovation remains the backbone of the company.
Since foundation in 1957, innovation has been our priority. We have always pioneered in the development of technologies and applications for handling corrosive fluids, from the 60's to the present time. Some examples:
 Process plastic pumps
 Reinforced plastic equipment
 First gas treatment installations
 Multiventuri type gas scrubbers
 High performance demisters
 Equipments manufactured in and PET
 Gas treatment installations using activated carbon
 Odor elimination by ozone
 Modular polyester covers
 Gas treatment by biological systems.
 Gas treatment using dry chemical systems
 Range of magnetic drive pumps
 Achievement of 9001
 Expansion of fan range
 Review and new range of centrifugal pumps
 certification of our products
 Biodesulphurization of biogas
 High performance adsorbents by activated alumina and zeolites
 Biotrickling
 Inorganic Biofilters for gas treatment
 Welders and laminators homologation
