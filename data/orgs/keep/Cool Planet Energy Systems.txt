Stories From the Soil
‘Stories From The Soil’ is a collaborative effort developed by the soil health company, Planet, and Tim Hammerich (the host of the Future of Agriculture podcast) to showcase and advance soil health solutions practiced by expert farmers, land stewards, and researchers. Learn from experts on the realities of soil health. Join the collaborative movement to innovate soil health technologies, increase productivity in the farm and green industry, and improve agricultural sustainability.

Completes 100th Independent Trial of Terra®
Completed the 100th Independent Trail of Terra®, the biochar-based soil amendment. The application of Terra® in the crop production trials have shown an improvement in soil health, and resulted in a greater-than-12 average increase in crop yield across a variety of crops and regions!
