As Teledyne's Central Research Laboratory, Company transitions technologies developed with contract R&investments from Government R&funding agencies into various businesses. We are a technology leader in high performance compound semiconductor devices and integrated circuits, ceramic and functional materials, efficient real-time information processing algorithms, and optical sensors and assemblies.
Sensors is a leader in high performance systems for military, space, astronomy, and commercial applications. Our products include infrared (IR) visible sensors, Read-Out Integrated Circuits (ROICs), and tactical cameras, camera electronics embedded with advanced algorithms, and laser eye and sensor protection devices filters.
