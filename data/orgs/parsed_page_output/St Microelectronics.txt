Our Mission & Vision
Our Mission & Vision
Every day, millions of people around the world benefit from ST’s products and technologies but few are aware of this because all of ST’s technology operates behind the scenes.
We are a leading semiconductor company, one of the most important and influential semiconductor players in the world and the largest European semiconductor company:
approximately 43,200 employees in the Group among which some 8,300 of our people work in R&D
As a successful, independent semiconductor manufacturer, we deliver leading-edge solutions to our customers worldwide, enabling them to be at the forefront of their markets.

Our Culture & Values
Our Culture of Diversity
We tap into the unique strength of each local unit and leverage those strengths worldwide.

Our Company has French and Italian roots and has grown global during the course of the years. Today over 40% of our top managers are from other cultures..

We hire people who have technical qualifications and we recruit solely on ability, regardless of gender, ethnicity, religion, age or disability. Women make up 39% of our workforce.
