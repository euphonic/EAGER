Values
To carry out our mission is a priority. For that reason actively promotes among its collaborators the following values:
- 
Respect to costumers:

History
History
Tecnium: Pioneers in the handling of corrosive fluids
Founded in 1957 in Manresa (Barcelona), born to meet a need in the industry: finding solutions to handling corrosive liquids and gases.
Innovation remains the backbone of the company.
50 INNOVATION
Since foundation in 1957, innovation has been our priority. We have always pioneered in the development of technologies and applications for handling corrosive fluids, from the 60's to the present time. Some examples:
1958
 Process plastic pumps
1962

 Polyester fans

.About us
– Casals Cardona Ind., is specialized in the manufacture of capital goods to handle corrosive fluids and equipment and installations for gas treatment.
Our task is to implement projects and constructions that meet the needs and expectations of our customers, in terms of reliability, competitiveness and quality, based on our long experience and under the most demanding international standards.
"Serving customers in a personalized, agile and warm, with a team with highly qualified technicians, is our highest priority."
We also want to continue to evolve and adapt with investment in research and development, which has meant that, since our beginnings in 1957, we have always been a leading company.
Our means of production are located in Manresa from our origins. At present it has a production area of 15,000 m2 spread over three buildings and an office of 1,200 m2. These facilities are available to those costumers who want to visit them, taking advantage of a good strategic location as well as ease of access.
Also has an extensive international network of collaborators and manufacturers that has allowed our business and products can be shipped worldwide, keeping production and transportation costs under control and ensuring the highest quality standards required by .
Our commitment as a company of national and international renown and be the best industry benchmark for customers who require a higher quality, expertise and service, motivates us to continue forward to this great project.
