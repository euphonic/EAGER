Who is Inc. was established in 1995 with a straightforward mission: to provide best payroll services available, and to do that by focusing on excellent customer service and affordable prices.
Today, over two decades later, our commitment to that original mission has resulted in meeting and exceeding needs of thousands of diverse business clients worldwide.
Our dedication to customer service is key reason why PayMaster, Inc. continues to grow in crowded payroll services industry. Unlike our national competitors, we will never refer to you by a client number. When you call our office, you will be greeted by a knowledgeable employee who will direct your call to right person, so it can be handled in most efficient and timely manner. If it any time you have a question or concern, no matter how large or small, we are here for you.
currently services a wide range of clients in a variety of industries, located in all 50 states, Puerto Rico, Virgin Islands, and Guam. These businesses have workforces ranging from one person to thousands of employees.
As our client base has grown over years, our services have also expanded beyond only payroll processing. In addition to best payroll solution in industry, we also offer some of most advanced Time & Labor Management, Services and Support, Tax Credit Processing, Employee Recruiting/Onboarding, and more.

Our Founder, Romeo Chicco
Romeo Chicco
Romeo Chicco is proud founder of and core of success of company. He is a dedicated family man who has been married to his lovely wife for 25 years and they have two beautiful children and two adorable pups. He resides in South Florida and is one of most hands on owners you will ever meet. He is strongly committed to both his family and business and takes both very seriously. He has been known to be at clients offices at 6 a.m. or at a business location late into evening, if that’s what a client needs or requests.
His strong character and personal and professional ethics have set tone for day to day operations of business. He doesn’t bring someone into family unless they exhibit same passion for doing right by clients as he has.
Romeo is also very active in payroll industry and sits on several boards, including Independent Payroll Providers Association and Payroll Fraud Protection Group. In 2016 he received recognition of Member of Year from Independent Payroll Providers Association, an organization he has been a charter member of since 1997. He also spends time volunteering as well as serves on board of a number of non-profit organizations.
With a deep-seated love for technology, a quick wit about him and ability to make everyone he comes in contact with feel welcomed and comfortable, Romeo is a pure joy to know and work with. He’s never too busy to offer assistance or give advice. Chances are, you’re not going to find a better out there if you tried.
