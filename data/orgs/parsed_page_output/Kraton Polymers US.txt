Who We Are
develops, manufactures and markets biobased  chemicals  and specialty that deliver exceptional value and enhance the lives of  people all over the world. As  a leading global producer of styrenic block co(SBC) and pine  chemicals, we manufacture high-performance materials that differentiate our customers’ products and meet multi-market needs.  Our global footprint, reliable supply, extensive  expertise and integrated portfolio of high-quality products enable our customers to push the boundaries of  performance to power the future of innovation.
has more than 800 customers across a diverse range of  end markets in over 70 countries worldwide. We are committed to providing the  superior product development, exceptional technical service, sustainable  solutions and reliable quality supply that gives you the innovation edge.
Vision
To be an admired  Fortune 500 specialty chemical company delivering exceptional value to our  shareholders, customers and employees.
Our vision sets the stage for us to continue redefining the specialty  chemical industry, just as we did over 50 years ago when we invented – and  the industry. Today, through our unique chemistry and commitment to  R&D, we are addressing market needs that include increasing  demand for higher performance and solving health  and environmental concerns. We are up to the challenge as we continue to  deliver compelling, innovative solutions that help our customers  differentiate their products, drive value for our shareholders and enrich the lives  of our employees – all of which are essential to our company’s continued growth.  Because at Kraton, we believe  a win-win strategy is the right way to do business.

Pioneering Innovation
Kraton’s legacy of innovations began in the 1950s, since we  were founded as part of Shell Chemical Company. Today, continues to  provide high-performance, sustainable solutions that deliver exceptional value  to customers, enabling them to make products that enhance the lives of people  all over the world. After more than half a century of innovation,  remains committed to redefining the industry through better chemistry.
