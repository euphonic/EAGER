Company
The company
Where Vision leads, others follow. For 75 years, Vision’s revolutionary optical and healthcare solutions have been pushing technological boundaries for customers around the world. Lens design has advanced considerably over the years, but one thing has remained unchanged: the dedication to quality and innovation for which Vision has become known.
Vision works tirelessly to understand the individual needs of its customers. From the latest lens designs and thinnest, most durable materials to award-winning anti-reflective coatings and advanced vision examination technologies, Vision invests heavily in research and development. The result is a world-renowned range of products providing total eye care.

History
Pioneers create history
was founded in the city of Hoya, Tokyo, 1941, by Shoichi and Shigeru Yamanaka, two visionary brothers. Originally a small manufacturer of optical lenses, the company saw opportunities in the emerging fields of healthcare and information technology.
Today, technology can also be found in medical camera lenses, Blu-ray players, smartphones, computers and televisions. For more than 75 years we have been driven by our dedication to innovate and use cutting-edge technology for providing the very finest eyeglass lenses to the market.
Learn more
1941 – Founded by Yamanaka brothers.
1958 – ventures into the eyeglass business with the manufacturing of lens press material.
1962 – Hoya's first original lens, “NEO”, is launched and its protection and anti-glare characteristics as well as a new curve design without comma aberration, are well received.
1970 – opens its first office in San Francisco.
1974 – Hoya's opens its first overseas lens manufacturing plant in Patumthani, Thailand.
1984 – The company is renamed and shifts its main business focus from “glass” to “light (optics)”.
1987 – launches it’s first intraocular (IOLs) lenses.
1991 – begins producing glass disks for computer hard drives.
2001 – launches Summit Pro and NuLux lenses that use EYRY, a high-index, plastic lens material.  Hoya's first soft intraocular lenses are launched. 
2007 – becomes a consolidated subsidiary of Hoya.
2008 – The companies merge. now operates in three fields: Information Technology, Eyecare and (lifecare and imaging).
2010 – Healthcare Co. Ltd. becomes Eye Care Division of Corporation. The company now operates in the business fields of Electronics, Imaging, Healthcare and Medical.
