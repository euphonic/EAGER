Toshiba/ExploraVision
Sponsored by and administered by the National Science Teachers Association, Toshiba/ExploraVision is the world’s largest science competition for K-12 students. Since 1992 the ExploraVision program has awarded more than $5.5 million (maturity value) to close to 350,000 participants. Up to $240,000 (maturity value) in savings bonds and products are awarded each year.
America Foundation
America Foundation is a private, endowed, not-for-profit grant-making organization. Established in 1990, the Foundation contributes to the quality of science and mathematics education in schools by investing in projects designed by classroom teachers to improve instruction for students in grades K-12.
