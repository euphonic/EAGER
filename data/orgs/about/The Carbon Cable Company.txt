About Us
Since 1989 
The Cable Company
 has been the 
world´s leading retailer of specialty audio cables
. But more than that our real specialty is providing high-level expertise on all aspects of high-performance audio. For our US customers, we can offer nearly every cable, powerline product, and resonance control product for 
in-home evaluation
 through our famous 
Lending Library service
.
Business Hours:
Monday through Friday from 10am to 6pm and Saturday from 11am to 5pm 
(Sat. showroom hours by appointment only)

Cables:
For our US customers we operate the Cable Library service offering home trial on over 60 brands of cables. Feedback from Library users drives our cable/component matching database. We can tell you what cables other audiophiles with your components have found to work best. There are many good cables, but only one optimal match for each system. Let us help you draw up your short list of cables for home audition. Learn more about The Cable Library in our "FAQ."  (
International Customers click here
) 
Power Line Products:
Another core specialty area at The Cable Company that are available to try at home through the Lending Library. For more information see the 
Power Line Conditioners
 section in our "FAQ."
Acoustic Treatment and Resonance Control:
 We are the masters of these important areas of high-performance audio. Our Acoustic Treatment manufacturers include Acoustic Systems, ASC Tube Traps, Harmonix, Golden Sound, Marigo Labs, RoomTune, Synergistic Research, and the Shakti Hallograph.
Accessories: 
We have dozens of brands of racks, stands and footers in our Accessories category. The resonance characteristics of your components and the platforms on which they are sitting will dictate best choices. We can help!
Used and Demo Component Specials:
Along with our 
Ultra Systems
 affiliate company, we offer equipment expertise and many components for in-home evaluation. Used and demo component specials appear in our 
Used Components
 database. For used cables you can jump to

Chord Company
Chord Company

Founded in 1985, the Chord Company mission has been to deliver the most neutral HiFi cabling, neither adding nor taking away from the music. Founder Sally Kennedy started the company in her home, building the very first cables in her kitchen! Quickly exceeding the capacity of each new production facility, Chord made its final move to its current Wiltshire UK location in 2004. Originally concentrated on building interconnects, Chord Company cables had early success with Cobra RCA and XLR interconnect cables followed by the Chord Chameleon analog cables- which are still in the line-up today and selling better than ever. 
Select Chord Company products are available for audition in your home and on your system through our 
Cable, Component and Hi-End Headphone Lending Library.

The Vibrapod Company has long offered affordable and effective, weight specific, isolators to improve the performance of audio components. They are particularly useful for use with stacked components, and the performance is substantially improved when they are combined with the Vibrapod Cones. 
Select Vibrapod Company products are available for audition in your home and on your system through our 
Cable, Component and Hi-End Headphone Lending Library.

THE CABLE COMPANY SINCE 1989
For over 25 years The Cable Company has been known as expert industry specialists in the audiophile, home audio / video, mobile (on-the-go) audio and Pro audio markets. Whether you are looking for cutting-edge emerging technology, practical easy-to-use products or have questions about a product, technology or consumer electronics industry in general… let us know, we’d love to talk with you and help in any way we can. All of the products that you will find on our site are products that have proven to be the best of the thousands of consumer electronics products we carry. Quite simply they are the peak performers and demonstrate the highest quality.
We are located in Union Square, New Hope, PA, next to the New Hope & Ivyland Railroad and not far from the Bucks County Playhouse, and Washington's Crossing. A short walk across the bridge brings you into Lambertville, NJ.
