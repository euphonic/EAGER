Companies
Weatherford is one of the largest multinational oilfield service companies.
Providing innovative solutions, technology and services to the oil and gas industry, the following entities are a part of the Weatherford group of companies.
OUR COMPANIES
Weatherford Laboratories
We expect more from ourselves so you can expect more from us.
Weatherford Laboratories combines an unsurpassed global team of geoscientists, engineers, technicians and researchers with the industry's most comprehensive, integrated laboratory services worldwide.
WDI
WDI has combined the construction of new rigs with the acquisition of strategic assets to form one of the oil and gas industry’s leading drilling contractors. The company has more than 60 years of experience and now deploys more than 100 rigs in key oil and gas areas worldwide.
Al-Shaheen Well Services Company (ASWSC)
Established in 2008, Al-Shaheen Well Services Company (ASWSC) is a Qatari joint venture between Al-Shaheen Energy Services (ASES) and Weatherford Holding BVI, a subsidiary of Weatherford International Ltd. (NYSE:WFT), one of the world’s largest oilfield service and equipment companies.
Weatherford Direct is the wholesale division of Weatherford’s Cased-Hole Completion Services. We specialize in the distribution of genuine Weatherford downhole equipment to service companies across the globe.
