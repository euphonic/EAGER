Company Overview
We are a UK based digital technology innovator with over 15 years of technical, commercial and operational expertise in the TV industry.
We specialise in analogue switch off and wide scale legacy set-top box upgrades, in addition to new pay TV products.  Our wide range of broadcast and OTT services enables operators to better monetise content and delivers a compelling user experience across all TV viewing platforms and cost-effective hardware.
What’s different about Inview?
 LIGHTWEIGHT TECHNOLOGY
We offer more for less on any set-top box and can upgrade deployed products with new UI & services
OPERATIONAL SUPPORT

COMMERCIAL EXPERIENCE
Our most recent projects include:
Deployment of a DTH ecosystem to 2nd largest pay TV operator in Mexico – Dish
Unified the UI, branding and services across leading pay TV operator in the Philippines, Cignal TV’s product range
Supplied the middleware for the American Forces Network’s new range of HD set-top boxes
Selected middleware provider for Ethiopian Digital Switchover
Government mandated DTT and DTH Free-To-Air platform and middleware for Nigeria including incremental pay TV, Push VOD and IP services, expected to reach 23 million homes. Aside from providing the technology, we are responsible for operating the entire service and driving analogue switch off in the country.
