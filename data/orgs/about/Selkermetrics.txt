SelkerMetrics specializes in planning, analysis, and design of environmental monitoring projects. Our team applies cutting-edge measurement and analysis solutions to provide clear and quantitative environmental information.
SelkerMetrics has created proprietary hardware and software that enables efficient and accurate characterization of water movement in a variety of environments.  Our equipment has been proven and refined in a wide variety of settings including rivers, marine coastal areas, and groundwater.
Applications have included numerous Superfund investigations and results have been published in peer-reviewed journals and submitted to state and national regulators.
All of our team spends time in the field, analyzing data, and most importantly, listening to our clients. Our process excels at translating data into easily understood, customer specific metrics to help you address your unique environmental challenges.
