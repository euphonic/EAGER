About Us
Founded in 2009 and headquartered in Houston, Rebellion Photonics offers the only camera in the world that can both identify and quantify gas releases in real time. Rebellion’s Gas Cloud Imaging (GCI) system utilizes proprietary hyperspectral imaging technology with advanced detection algorithms to pinpoint the source and measure the volume and concentration of leaks. GCI is the next generation for leak detection and monitoring at all points of the petrochemical industry.
