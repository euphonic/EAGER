About Authenex®
Authenex®, founded in 2000, is a venture capital funded company based in Mountain View, CA, with engineering and marketing resources in Taiwan, R.O.C.
Our two-factor authentications, secure mobility, and secure mobile storage solution products are deployed in Fortune 100 and 500 companies as well as in many small and medium sized businesses.
Since Authenex® owns the technology for their firmware, software and hardware (all patent protected) they can provide the best price/performance in the market today.
