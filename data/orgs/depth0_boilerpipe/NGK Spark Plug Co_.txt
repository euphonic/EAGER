ORIGINAL EQUIPMENT
NGK Spark Plugs (U.S.A.), Inc. is the largest OE supplier and manufacturer of spark plugs, oxygen sensors and A/F sensors for domestic and import vehicles in North America. With over 50 years of automotive expertise, NGK’s family of automotive products are driven by extreme dedication to innovation, performance and quality. For decades, NGK application engineers in Wixom, Michigan have collaborated with auto manufacturers to provide technologies fit for the vehicles of the future.
NGK Spark Plugs (U.S.A.), Inc is committed to be the premier partner to our customers, while maintaining original equipment standards for the highest durability and operational performance
