Formed   in 2009, Global OLED Technology, LLC ("GOT") owns a portfolio   containing more than two thousand patents and pending patent   applications, including foundational patents, in the area of Organic   Light Emitting Diode (“OLED”) technology. GOT aims to contribute to the   continued development of the OLED industry in areas such as OLED display   and OLED lighting applications. GOT’s patent portfolio encompasses a   heritage of innovation that began in the early 1980’s when pioneering   OLED intellectual property was created and incubated within Eastman   Kodak Company’s research and development labs.
