Silicor Materials is a global leader in the production of solar silicon. The company is headquartered San Jose, California with a Research and Development team located in Berlin, Germany.

Silicor produces the industry’s lowest-cost solar silicon while delivering performance on par with silicon produced via the standard “Siemens” method.
While both the Siemens and Silicor processes begin with metallurgical-grade silicon (MG-Si) and end with purified silicon for solar cells and modules, it’s what happens in between that sets Silicor apart.

ENVIRONMENTALLY FRIENDLY MANUFACTURING
Polysilicon manufacturing can be one of the most environmentally hazardous product processes in the solar PV value chain, partially because the Siemens method requires large amounts of volatile trichlorosilane gas. By contrast, Silicor’s process requires no handling of hazardous gases, improving safety for both workers and the surrounding environment.
