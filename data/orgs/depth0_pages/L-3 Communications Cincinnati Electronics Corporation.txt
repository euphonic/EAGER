Who We Are
A Proven Developer
L3 Space &  Sensors is an established pioneer in space exploration and military/defense technology. A leader in high-technology and engineering services, L3 Space &  Sensors is world renowned for its expertise in the areas of infrared detection and space and missile electronics. As a first-class engineering and production company, L3 Space &  Sensors is engaged in the design, development, and manufacture of highly-sophisticated electronics equipment that is used in a variety of product areas for government, civil and commercial applications.
