            Saint-Gobain Performance Plastics, a Leader in Polymer Product Technology          
Saint-Gobain Performance Plastics
 is the world's leading producer of engineered, high-performance polymer products, and serves virtually every major industry across the globe. Saint-Gobain Performance Plastics businesses support these key industries with advanced technology polymer products for the most demanding applications. Backed by a proud heritage of product innovation, technological expertise and market leadership, Saint-Gobain Performance Plastics is dedicated to working with its customers to solve today's application issues and the challenges that lie ahead.
