Innovation Without Boundaries
®
In 2014, BRANDT Consolidated, Inc. and iHammer LLC teamed up to form the joint venture, BRANDT iHammer, which is dedicated to agronomic research and product development. BRANDT iHammer’s mission is to develop new technologies that help plants use water and nutrients more efficiently, and help its customers achieve better agronomic results. The agronomic research is focused on glycoside sugar molecules, photosynthesis and carbon flow within plants.
