Events in Warsaw, Indiana
Live in the Orthopedic Capital of the World
®
Thinking of relocating to Warsaw or have you just moved here? Warsaw, Indiana and the surrounding region have a LOT to offer. While Warsaw is a small town in north-central Indiana, it is centrally located between several larger metropolitan areas, and offers many big city amenities along with its small town charm. Residents enjoy affordable living, excellent schools and many other assets one would not expect from a Midwestern city of around 15,000 people. Work in the Orthopedic Capital of the World
®
Warsaw, Indiana is best known as 
The Orthopedic Capital of the World
®
, but it also offers a wide array of employment opportunities for individuals of all skill levels. Kosciusko County offers positions in the agribusiness and automotive industries, in addition to the high-tech medical device companies that are headquartered here. Play in the Orthopedic Capital of the World
®
Whether you enjoy indoor or outdoor recreation, Warsaw, Indiana has something for you. We have miles of bike and pedestrian trails, three lakes, world-class dining and a professional theatre in the round – and that is just within the city limits of Warsaw and Winona Lake! Our region has abundant recreational assets that people of all ages and interests can enjoy. To learn more about what it’s like to play in Warsaw, Indiana, visit:
