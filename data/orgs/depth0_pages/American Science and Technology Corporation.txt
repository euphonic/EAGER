American Science and Technology Corporation (AST) is a full service shared piloting facility, available to industry and dedicated to helping our clients develop innovative biorefinery and chemical technologies to convert lignocellulosic biomass into high-value, bio-based chemicals and products.  
The AST facility, located in Wausau (WI), operates from laboratory level to multi-ton scale and is equipped with a wide range of material handling and biomass processing equipment to provide a unique opportunity for collaboration to accelerate the advancement of the bio-based economy.
