DISCOVER NEW PRODUCTS
OSFP Connectors and Cable Assemblies
Our new Octal Small Form Factor Pluggable (OSFP) portfolio uses innovative thermal heat sink technology for superior thermal performance and signal integrity at speeds up to 400 Gbps.
HIRSCHMANN MOBILITY Remote Tuner Module (RTM)
RTM provides automotive manufacturers with radio tuner technology that concentrates central radio functions at the vehicle’s rear. This simplifies the antenna system architecture and minimizes system costs.
124 Position Sliver Connectors and Cable Assemblies
This high-density solution is designed for PCIe Gen 4 and Gen 5, Ethernet 56G and SAS-5. 124 position Sliver connectors and cable assemblies also enable better cable management than previously possible.
NanoRF Module and Contacts
This offers double the density of VITA 67 RF modules for VPX embedded computing. Its high-frequency nanominiature coax contact includes smaller contacts and higher RF contact density within a multi-position module, for smaller packaging.
ParaByte Fiber Optic Transceivers
This high-density optical transceiver features a parallel 12-fiber ribbon cable and supports speeds up to 12.5 GB/s per channel and temperatures between -40C to +100C.
M12 PCB/Panel Connectors 8/12 Pin
These compact connectors offer high-pin density to deliver efficient, high-quality, and reliable signals and data transmission. 
Low Profile Flexible Printed Circuit (FPC) Connectors
Save space with one of the lowest profiles in the market and improve assembly efficiency by having the actuator open at delivery, enabling one finger operation.
BUCHANAN WireMate Two-Piece Poke-In Series
Reduce installation time with these easy-to-use poke-in connectors featuring a simple lever design that releases one wire at a time.
